How to style a Quiff

Styling a quiff involves creating a voluminous and upward-swept hairstyle. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair, then apply a volumizing mousse or styling product to add texture and hold. Use a blow dryer to lift the hair at the roots while directing it upward with a round brush. Once the hair is mostly dry, switch to a comb or your fingers to lift and shape the quiff. Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to define the texture and hold the shape in place. You can experiment with the height and angle of the quiff to suit your preferences. Finish with a light hairspray to ensure the style stays in place throughout the day.
